Discovery at Aspen by Sophie Ruskay

Book description:

"Discovery at Aspen" by Sophie Ruskay is a novel written in the mid-20th century. The story centers around a young girl named Judy Lurie who is about to spend a summer in Aspen, Colorado, with her musically devoted parents. As she grapples with her feelings of being an outsider in a world surrounded by music, she begins to question her own identity and future amidst her family's expectations.
